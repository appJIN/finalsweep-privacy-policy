Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

FinalSweep is a daily essentials checklist app developed by appJIN. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

FinalSweep is designed to work as a simple checklist app. We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, address, photos, contacts, or location.

The app may store your checklist preferences, selected language, completed items, custom lists, and app settings locally on your device. This information is used only to provide the app’s core functionality and is not sent to our server.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

FinalSweep may display ads using Google AdMob or other advertising services. These third-party services may collect and process certain information, such as device information, advertising ID, app usage data, crash information, and approximate location, to provide, measure, and improve ads.

Third-party advertising providers may use cookies, identifiers, or similar technologies according to their own privacy policies. We encourage users to review Google’s privacy policy and AdMob related privacy information for more details.

3. How We Use Information

The app uses information for the following purposes:

  • To save checklist progress and app preferences on the device
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To show advertisements, if ads are enabled
  • To maintain app stability and performance

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or directly share your personal information with anyone. However, third-party services such as Google AdMob may collect and process data according to their own policies when ads are displayed in the app.

5. Data Storage and Security

Checklist data and preferences are stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, locally stored app data may be deleted. We take reasonable steps to keep the app safe, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

FinalSweep is intended for general users and is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

7. User Choices

You can clear app data or uninstall the app at any time from your device settings. You may also manage ad personalization through your device or Google account settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
